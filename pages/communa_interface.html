Communa App

«Communa. Find the neighbors of your dreams» is an application that helps the creative class solve the housing issue by finding the ideal roommate using a flexible questionnaire.

Problems

— limited budget;
— it’s scary to look for an apartment alone;
— household inconsistency with neighbors.

Decision

— live together and share the rent;
— to look for an apartment together or to move in;
— detailed questionnaire → correspondence acquaintance.

How does it work?

1. Fill out the questionnaire — describe your everyday habits and other important points.
2. You pick up a neighbor — you set filters, read questionnaires and like the profile.
3. You get acquainted — you correspond with a potential neighbor, discuss options and arrange a meeting.

For many people, an apartment seems to be an indivisible space — you either live alone, or with your family/partner. In our application, we show a different approach when the apartment is divided into several people, which makes life more diverse, simple and interesting.

Therefore, the identity is based on the metaphor of a commune placed in rooms.

The application repeats the mechanics of dating apps. In it, you can not only choose the neighbors that suit you according to a detailed questionnaire, but also create a chat of several people and create a «commune».

You can use the «Commune» even from a computer, since the interface of the web version has been worked out and the main functionality has been preserved.
