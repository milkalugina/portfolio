Communa App

«Communa. Find the neighbors of your dreams» is an application that helps the creative class solve the housing issue by finding the ideal roommate using a flexible questionnaire.

Problems

— limited budget;
— it’s scary to look for an apartment alone;
— household inconsistency with neighbors.

Decision

— live together and share the rent;
— to look for an apartment together or to move in;
— detailed questionnaire → correspondence acquaintance.

How does it work?

1. Fill out the questionnaire — describe your everyday habits and other important points.
2. You pick up a neighbor — you set filters, read questionnaires and like the profile.
3. You get acquainted — you correspond with a potential neighbor, discuss options and arrange a meeting.
