Good deeds

«Good Deeds» is a media service that helps volunteers and activists to find an event or project in which they would like to participate.

How does it work?

Organization (NGO) — publishes news and cases, finds volunteers.
Volunteers — read the news and find cases of interest.

Mission

Our mission is to find volunteers for every cause, making the world a kinder and better place.

Target audience

— For those who do volunteer work in their free time;
— for NGOs and people working there.

Positioning

— We help NGOs save time and money;
— We quickly find volunteers for NGO projects;
— We help volunteers find interesting projects.

We show the brand as an association of volunteers — people of different ages, having different values and interests, but connected by one idea — the hope for «good in the world», as well as the understanding that any good deed is important.

DD is an abbreviation of «Good Deeds» in Russian. Combining letters as unity and color difference as a division into different spheres.

We have designed two landing pages: one for volunteers, the second for organizations.

Also, a full-fledged interface was developed, a design system and a UI kit were created. The video shows two main scenarios for users.
