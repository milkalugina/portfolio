Different weather motivates for different actions, cloudy — helps to fall asleep and relax, sunny — helps most to concentrate on work.

How

Using a neural network. The lens, which is based on a thin display, replaces the image, sky, light and reflections with those that occur in good weather.

Target audience based on survey results

People who are 60 percent dependent on the weather and would like to change it if possible. Emotional state is important for them.

Job Stories

When the weather is bad and the mood deteriorates, I don’t want to do anything. I want to control my condition, stop being weather-dependent. Thanks to wearing this accessory, my performance has improved, my emotional state does not depend on the weather outside.

CusDev results

— more often it’s young people
— people like different weather
— everyone would like to change the weather whenever possible
— most people are mood-dependent on the weather
— quality and design are important
— main functions
— change the weather and improve mood
— everyone wants to read reviews

I didn’t want the product to have any special futuristic design. It should be an accessory for everyday wear, no more.
