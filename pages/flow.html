Flow App

An application that shows you all the coworking spaces near you + gives you the opportunity to book a place for yourself / company.

Problems

There is not a single coworking aggregator that is relevant on the territory of Russia, including Moscow, and also most coworking sites do not have an online reservation. Most often, people are looking for a place to work through social networks/acquaintances.

Job to be done

— People hire my product to search and book coworking spaces.
— Every time they are looking for a place to work, they log into the app.
— Other products for this work are AndCo, Fast Office, Worka, Opus.
— But my product will always do this job, because it will be relevant in our geography, in which there are no such projects yet.

Job Stories

When it is inconvenient for me to work at home or in coffee shops, I want to find an equipped place for productive problem solving so that my efficiency does not fall.

As a result of the research, we learned that filters are needed (coworking features are important to many), in addition to subscriptions, one—time visits are needed — a lot of people go a couple of times a month, and also that the rating is important for visitors.

The application repeats the mechanics of dating apps. In it, you can not only choose the neighbors that suit you according to a detailed questionnaire, but also create a chat of several people and create a «commune».

You can use the «Commune» even from a computer, since the interface of the web version has been worked out and the main functionality has been preserved.
