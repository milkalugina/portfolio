Chronos Concept

Chronos is a store of goods from the future. We go to the future in a time machine, buy there, and resell in the present. However, because of our commercial activities, there is a butterfly effect...

Project features

There are an infinite number of new technologies in the infinite future. With the instant delivery feature, it’s much easier to buy online.

Job to be done

— Promising and young;
— Live in big cities;
— They work in the digital sphere;
— Take care of yourself;
— Interested in future amateurs;
— unknown and new.

Job Stories

When I was sitting on Instagram, I saw an advertisement and wanted to know what «the store of the future» means. Therefore, I flip through the product pages to satisfy the interest that has arisen.

Basic techniques in identity:the butterfly effect is like a glitch and running time is like mercury.

The interface is designed in a brutalist style. There are also several basic scenarios designed: product selection, purchase process and error page.
